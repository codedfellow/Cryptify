Welcome to Cryptify

Cryptify is a powerful and user-friendly web application that makes encryption and decryption simple and secure. Built with Angular and C#, Cryptify lets you protect your messages using industry-standard algorithms like AES, alongside educational ciphers such as Caesar and XOR, and the versatile Base64 encoding. Whether you're safeguarding sensitive data or exploring the art of cryptography, Cryptify has you covered.

Why Cryptify?

  • Multiple Algorithms: Choose from AES for robust security, Caesar and XOR for classic cryptography, or Base64 for encoding needs.
  • Intuitive Interface: Our sleek Angular-powered UI makes it easy to encrypt and decrypt text with just a few clicks.
  • Secure Backend: Powered by ASP.NET Core, Cryptify ensures reliable and safe cryptographic operations.
  • Educational and Practical: Learn how ciphers work while experimenting with real-world encryption techniques.
